2024 Highlights

Foundation Year

Construction & Opening

Construction commenced March 8, 2024 and concluded on June 15, 2024 — a 3-month build delivering a state-of-the-art 345 sq m rehabilitation facility at Robinsons Metro East, Pasig.

Multidisciplinary Team Built

By year-end, SCEI assembled 43+ professionals across 9 disciplines — OT, PT, SLP, SPED, Medical, Psychology, Orthosis & Prosthesis, and support staff — setting the standard for comprehensive care.

97.23% Overall Performance

Despite being in its first year, the clinic achieved 97.23% of projected targets. Occupational Therapy exceeded projections by +24.75%, while O&P services added unplanned revenue.

Who We Are

Identity & Purpose

Sapphire Clinics East, Inc. (SCEI) operates Sandbox Clinic East — a premier rehabilitation facility committed to delivering accessible, evidence-based healthcare to the Metro East community and beyond.

Mission

To become the leading rehabilitation institution in the Philippines.

Vision

To optimize the quality of life through accessible, excellent, and evidence-based rehabilitation services.

Our Purpose

Why We Exist

Clinical Excellence

To provide the highest quality of Physical Therapy, Occupational Therapy, Speech Therapy, and all services we offer to our patients.

Professional Hub

To be the destination of young, driven, motivated, competent, and like-minded professionals who want to further raise the standard of practice in the medical and allied health profession.

Community Impact

To help the community by providing accessible and high-quality healthcare, thus creating happy, productive, and healthy people. Aspires to create a healthy and safe environment for the future generation.

Our Brand Promise

Our institution aspires to be the gold standard in rehabilitation care in the Philippines, setting a benchmark for quality, accessibility, and evidence-based practices. As a hub for professional excellence and training, we aim to inspire and cultivate the next generation of healthcare leaders. Beyond individual care, our goal is to contribute meaningfully to society by creating healthier and more resilient communities.

Department Performance

2024 Results

In 2024, Sandbox Clinic East demonstrated commendable performance across multiple departments, achieving 97.23% of the projected target. Below is a detailed assessment of each department's performance, accomplishments, and challenges.

Overall Performance vs. Target
97.23%

2024 marked a year of foundational growth and valuable lessons. With strategic adjustments and resource optimization, SCEI is well-positioned for greater success in 2025.

Occupational Therapy

OT Services

+24.75%

The OT Department exceeded expectations, surpassing projections by +24.75%. This outstanding performance reflects the department's effectiveness in meeting client demands and leveraging its reputation. Continued focus on service quality and partnerships will sustain this momentum.

Exceeded TargetTop Performing Dept
Physical Therapy

PT Services

80.63%

The PT Department achieved 80.63% of its target. This solid performance was supported by the availability of advanced equipment, which enhanced marketing efforts and attracted clients. The ongoing accreditation process with several HMOs is expected to further bolster sales once finalized.

Solid PerformanceHMO Accreditation Pending
Speech-Language Pathology

SLP Services

38.05%

Revenue achieved 38.05% of projection, primarily due to the limited pool of available SLPs (few schools offer speech therapy programs nationwide). The recent licensure exam in November 2024 and forthcoming oath-taking in January 2025 restricted new hiring. An increase in recruitment is anticipated in 2025.

Staffing LimitedImproving in 2025
SPED Services

SPED Department

38.45%

SPED Services generated 38.45% of target, reflecting lower demand in the clinic's area. Services were restructured to better align with local demand. The proposed budgeted income statement for 2025 already reflects the revised sales expectations from this department.

Restructured for 2025Lower Local Demand
Medical Consultation

Medical Services

The successful recruitment of several developmental pediatricians significantly enhanced service offerings and boosted sales. The inclusion of registered psychologists shortly after clinic operations commenced further strengthened the department's capacity to meet client needs, contributing positively to overall revenue.

Strong RevenueDev Peds Key Driver
NEW: Orthosis & Prosthesis

O&P Services

Initially unplanned, Orthosis and Prosthesis Services were introduced to diversify the clinic's offerings and complement existing services, particularly for pediatric physical therapy patients. Despite being a new addition, this service contributed significantly in revenue, showcasing its potential to enhance overall sales and service integration.

Unplanned ServiceSignificant Revenue

Leveraging the robust health network previously established by Sandbox BGC, the East branch successfully expanded its multidisciplinary team by recruiting physicians from a variety of specialized fields. The inclusion of diverse medical disciplines enhances the clinic's ability to provide comprehensive, patient-centered care and ensures patients receive tailored treatment plans — further solidifying SCEI's reputation for excellence.

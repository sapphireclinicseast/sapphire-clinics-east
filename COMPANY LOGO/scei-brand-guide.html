01 — Brand Story

Who We Are &
What We Stand For

Sapphire Clinics East, Inc. (SCEI) is a rehabilitation-focused corporation and an associate of Sapphire Clinics Incorporated. SCEI currently operates two clinic branches under the Sandbox Clinic brand — Sandbox Clinic East and Sandbox Clinic Greenhills — delivering world-class, holistic rehabilitative care and professional excellence across Metro Manila. The Sandbox Clinic brand is owned by Sapphire Clinics Incorporated; SCEI operates its clinics as a licensed associate under that brand.

Vision

To lead the field of rehabilitation medicine in the Philippines by providing world-class, innovative, and holistic rehabilitative care and pioneering advancements in education and training.

Mission

To deliver exceptional rehabilitative services and cutting-edge treatments, while fostering education and training to equip the next generation of healthcare professionals, ensuring quality care and patient satisfaction.

Big Hairy Audacious Goal (BHAG)

To be the #1 market leader in the rehabilitation space
in the Philippines within 10 years.

Strategic Framework

The HEALTH Goals

Our six strategic pillars — each pillar also shapes how our brand communicates, looks, and feels.

H

One Operational Excellence

Optimize operations and implement state-of-the-art digitalization to streamline patient management and care delivery.

E

Xpand Market Presence

Launch targeted campaigns, open new clinics in strategic locations, and develop a comprehensive online presence.

Achieve annual revenue growth of at least 10% while ensuring sustainable profit margins and affordable care.

Implement advanced therapeutic modalities and achieve a patient satisfaction score of 90% or higher.

Ealthy Growth Initiatives

Diversify funding sources, explore new revenue streams, and create a social arm for underserved demographics.

03 — Color System

05 — Brand Voice & Tone

How We Sound

Our brand voice is the audible expression of who we are. Rooted in our vision to lead rehabilitation medicine in the Philippines, we speak with authority, warmth, and optimism — always centering the patient's journey.

🏆

Authoritative

We speak from a position of clinical expertise and evidence-based practice. We own our role as future market leaders without arrogance.

🤝

Compassionate

Behind every session is a person with goals, fears, and a family. We lead with empathy and patient-centered language in all communications.

💡

Innovative

We embrace cutting-edge modalities and technology. Our tone reflects forward-thinking confidence — pioneering, not following.

🌏

Inclusive

From pediatric SPED to adult rehabilitation to orthosis fabrication — we serve a wide spectrum. Our language is accessible and free of unnecessary medical jargon.

📈

Aspirational

Aligned with our BHAG, we communicate a vision of growth and possibility — for our patients, our team, and the Philippines' rehab landscape.

✅

Trustworthy

Transparent about our services, honest in our messaging. We build trust through consistent, reliable, and professional communication.

Voice in Practice

✓ Use "Your recovery journey starts here — with a team that's fully invested in your progress."
✗ Avoid "We offer services for rehabilitation patients at our clinic location."
✓ Use "Backed by world-class training and innovative modalities, our therapists bring the best of global rehabilitation science to your doorstep."
✗ Avoid "We are a clinic that does therapy. We have different types of therapy available."
✓ Use "Rehab for Every Age, Every Stage — experience the Sapphire Clinics East standard of care."
✗ Avoid "We are a clinic that does rehab. Come visit us for your therapy needs."
SCEI Core Tagline

"Rehab for Every Age, Every Stage."

This tagline communicates SCEI's commitment to serving the full spectrum of rehabilitation needs — from pediatric SPED to adult orthosis fabrication and beyond. It is inclusive, memorable, and directly aligned with the vision of leading rehabilitation medicine in the Philippines. Use this tagline across all SCEI corporate communications, clinic signage, digital assets, and patient-facing materials.

07 — Imagery

Photography &
Visual Style

Every image we publish should reinforce our brand promise: world-class care delivered with warmth, precision, and hope.

📷

Authentic Moments

Prioritize real therapist-patient interactions over posed stock photography. Candid, in-session imagery communicates genuine care.

🎨

Color Grading

Photos should have a cool-warm balance with slight teal toning in shadows. Avoid heavy filters. Keep skin tones natural and vibrant.

💫

Lighting

Clean, bright, clinical-yet-warm. Natural light preferred for patient photos. Even studio lighting for professional headshots and equipment.

👨‍👩‍👧

Subject Matter

Show the full patient journey: assessment, active therapy, achievement moments. Represent the diversity of our patient population across all ages.

🏥

Facility Shots

Showcase the modern, well-equipped clinic environment. Clean lines, teal accents, state-of-the-art therapy equipment, and multisensory spaces.

🚫

Avoid

Over-edited stock images, generic "hospital" aesthetics, overly clinical cold imagery, and any imagery that shows patients in distress without context.

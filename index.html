Who We Are

About Sapphire Clinics East, Inc.

Sapphire Clinics East, Inc. (SCEI) is dedicated to optimizing functional mobility, effective communication, and inclusive care — backed by evidence-based rehabilitation. SCEI operates branches under the Sandbox Clinic brand.

🎯 SCEI Mission
By optimizing quality of life through accessible, excellent, and evidence-based rehabilitation services. Optimize functional mobility in each individual, enable effective communication to all, promote inclusivity in the society, advance clinical research, and foster excellence in the professional community.
👁 SCEI Vision
To become the leading rehabilitation institution in the Philippines — a global leader in providing accessible, excellent, and evidence-based habilitation and rehabilitation services to help improve the overall health of society.
🚀 Our BHAG — Big Hairy Audacious Goal
To be the #1 market leader in the rehabilitation space in the Philippines in 10 years.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Sandbox Clinic.

About Sandbox Clinic
What is Sandbox Clinic? +
Sandbox Clinic is a healthcare hub dedicated to optimizing functional mobility, fostering effective communication, and promoting inclusivity in society. We specialize in evidence-based habilitation and rehabilitation services aimed at improving overall health and well-being.
Where is Sandbox Clinic located? +
We have two branches:

Sandbox Clinic – East Branch: Level 4, Robinsons Metro East, Marcos Highway, Brgy. Dela Paz, Santolan, Pasig

Sandbox Clinic – Greenhills Branch: Level 8, GH Tower Offices, South Drive, Ortigas Avenue, Greenhills, San Juan City
What services do you offer? +
Both branches offer: Medical Consultations, Occupational Therapy, Physical Therapy, Speech Language Therapy, Special Education Classes, Psychotherapy, and Orthosis & Prosthesis Fabrication. Please visit the Branches section for full details.
For Patients
How do I book an appointment? +
You can book an appointment by calling each branch at the contact details provided in our Contact section, or by sending a message on our Facebook or Instagram pages.
Do you accept walk-in patients? +
Yes, we accept walk-ins, subject to the availability of our staff. However, we recommend scheduling an appointment in advance to ensure timely service.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and select digital payment platforms. For insurance coverage and HMO payments, please check with your provider or contact us directly.
Can I access my medical records online? +
Yes, registered patients can access their medical records. Please check with each branch at our contact details for more information.
For Professionals
How can I join Sandbox Clinic as a health professional? +
We welcome applications from passionate healthcare professionals. Please visit our Careers section to learn more about open positions. You may also send your application to:

East: hr.sandboxcliniceast@gmail.com
Greenhills: hr.sandboxclinicgh@gmail.com
Does Sandbox Clinic offer training or mentorship programs? +
Yes, we provide mentorship and continuous professional development programs for our team and partner professionals. Please contact any branch to inquire about available programs.
What are the benefits of partnering with Sandbox Clinic? +
Benefits include: state-of-the-art facilities, competitive compensation with performance incentives, exclusive training opportunities, career growth pathways, collaborative professional environment, and comprehensive administrative support. See our Careers section for full details.
Compliance & Policies
How does Sandbox Clinic ensure patient privacy and data security? +
We adhere to strict confidentiality and data protection regulations, particularly the Data Privacy Act of 2012. Our systems are designed to safeguard all patient information.
Are your services compliant with local and national regulations? +
Yes, Sandbox Clinic operates fully in compliance with regulations set by the Professional Regulation Commission, Securities and Exchange Commission, Bureau of Internal Revenue, and the local government units where our branches are located.
